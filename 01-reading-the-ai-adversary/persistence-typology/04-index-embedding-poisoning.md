# Reading Index and Embedding Poisoning: The Strategic Actor's Long Game

**Status: [OPEN] hypothesis. Stub.**

Part 4 of the Persistence Typology series. Same structure as part 1.

The claim this piece will develop: corrupting the retrieval layer (poisoned documents in the RAG corpus, manipulation in embedding space; OWASP LLM04 and LLM08) is the high corner of the typology. It is slow, hard to attribute, and it shapes outcomes across many future queries and users rather than compromising one session. It is the closest thing on this surface to an information-operations capability rather than an access capability, and it reveals an actor optimizing for influence at scale over immediate access: the most strategic and the most nation-state-shaped of the four mechanisms. The poisoned document is not an exploit. It is a narrative with a delivery mechanism.
