# Reading Token and OAuth Persistence: The Mature Actor's Foothold

**Status: [OPEN] hypothesis. Stub.**

Part 3 of the Persistence Typology series. Same structure as part 1.

The claim this piece will develop: persisting at the identity layer (an unrevoked task token, an OAuth grant that outlives the session, a delegation chain that keeps a foothold) reveals the mature-actor insight that the durable foothold is the credential, not the content. Content-level tricks get cleaned up; identity-level access survives until someone audits grants. The natural framework home is MITRE ATT&CK rather than ATLAS, and that is itself the tell: this is established intrusion tradecraft arriving on a new surface, which marks the actor as one who brought it with them. High sophistication, patient, deniable.
